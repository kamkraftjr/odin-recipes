Dave soup

Dave soup is literally tasting derptonia in your mouth

This is a fantastic mix of flavours that i definitely recommend any human or bird person try

no preds tho, you guys arent allowed to try it.

Ingrediants:

  • 1/2tsp of love
  • 6g of Dave
  • 3 pinchs of geek Derp
  • 8 shovels of wen
  • 2mg of magic derpbird juice

Steps:

  1. get a bowl
  2. fill bowl with 6g of Dave
  3. then add 3 pinches of geek derp
  4. now mix together until smooth
  5. now add 2mg of magic derpbird juice and the 1/2tsp of love
  6. now stir again until not too grainy
  7. then add the 8 shovels of wen
  8. finnaly put the bowl on your car bonnet and wait 4 days then eat

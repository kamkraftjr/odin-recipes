Snail cake

snail cake literally taste like a snail cake but better

Snail cake is a revoultionary upgrade from previous snail cakes since this snail cake is made with derp magic so it taste so much better

Its just snail but when mixed with derp magic it becomes a derp snail (see image) and it taste lovely

Ingrediants:

  • 1 snail
  • 7tsp of derp magic juice
  • 9ml of lemonade
  • 4g of cocain (optional)

Steps:

  1. (the optional step) snort all 4g of cocain
  2. now get a plate
  3. get the snail and cut a small hole in its side
  4. add the 7tsp of derp magic juice and watch as it turns into a tasy derp snail cake
  5. now put the 9ml of lemonade over the top
  6. finnaly put it in the oven and let it cook at 650* for 6 hours then eat
  7. ENJOY
